## Soy
- Rating: ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️
- Product tested: *Bjorg - Soja Léger*
    - -> Light Soy
- Verdict:
    - Good level of sweetness
    - Not too strong soy flavor (which I personally don't enjoy)
    - Nutritionally similar to milk (light version)
